Star Wars Rankings

Ranking all Star Wars movies from best to worst

Star Wars: Underworld
Resume



  1. Revenge of the Sith
    • Emotional story
    • Visually stunning
    • Epic action sequences

  2. Empire Strikes Back
    • Compelling story
    • One of the greatest twists in cinema history

  3. Rogue One: A Star Wars Story
    • Compelling, morally ambiguous characters
    • Beautiful cinematography
    • The best space battle in all of Star Wars

  4. Return of the Jedi
    • Darth Vader character arc
    • Fun action
    • Ewoks are way too OP

  5. Attack of the Clones
    • Interesting story
    • Sadly suffers from pretty bad dialogue
    • Amazing final battle

  6. A New Hope
    • Get's credit for being the first Star Wars
    • Classic hero's journey
    • Predictable but fun

  7. Solo: A Star Wars Story
    • Different than other Star Wars movies
    • Explores the criminal element of Star Wars
    • Suprisingly good acting
    • Lack-luster finale

  8. The Phantom Menace
    • Criminally underrated
    • Good action but low stakes
    • Jar Jar Binks can be annoying

  9. The Force Awakens
    • Basically a remake of A New Hope
    • Doesn't do anything new
    • Beautiful production design

  10. The Last Jedi
    • Constantly breaks previously established lore
    • Undoes character development of the Original Trilogy characters
    • One of its three plotlines doesn't manifest into anything important to the story

  11. Rise of Skywalker
    • Feels forced and rushed
    • Sloppy storytelling; things merely happen because the plot demands it
